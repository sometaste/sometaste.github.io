About ODAC

Pei Hwa Outdoor Adventure Club (ODAC) offers students a wide range of outdoor activities and training programs designed to develop skills and inspire growth. We foster perseverance, discipline, and an appreciation for nature and our urban environment.

  • Outdoor skills training
  • Teamwork and leadership development
  • Physical fitness and mental resilience
  • Fun and challenging experiences
